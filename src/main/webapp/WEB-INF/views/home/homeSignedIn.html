Welcome to YouVersity!

As technology develops, there is a growing opportunity to gain more knowledge from the World Wide Web. YouVersity seeks to further the education experience by making it easy to collect and share online educational videos from YouTube and share them with others either through private playlists or as a class.


Features:

  • Having a hard time scanning through the same long videos again and again for information? You can create playlists of YouTube videos with customized timestamps. Now getting that one equation or model is a lot easier!
  • Share your own playlists with other users for study sessions, or just to lend a helping hand!
  • Classroom instructors, you create and moderate special class groups where you can share your educational playlists with all group members!
