Welcome to EduTube!

As technology develops, there is a growing opportunity to gain more knowledge from the World Wide Web. EduTube seeks to further the education experience by making it easy to collect and share online educational videos from YouTube and share them with others either through private playlists or as a class.


Features:

  • Create playlists of YouTube videos
  • Share playlists with other users
  • For instructors: Create class groups which you can share playlists with as a group
